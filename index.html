Australian National Championship 2025-2026

During December 2025, FVT competed at the Australian National Championships in Adelaide to fight for the spot to go to the World Championship in St. Louis, Missouri. Although FVT did not qualify, the team was very happy to place second overall in qualification matches, making it to the semifinals!
